About Hagen Blacksmithing

Featured in local North Dakota media including Agweek and Minot Daily News, Hagen Blacksmithing has been creating exceptional ironwork that combines timeless craftsmanship with modern functionality. Each piece is hand-forged using time-honored methods passed down through generations.

As part of North Dakota's North Central Region art and cultural destinations, our workshop specializes in custom ironwork, decorative pieces, and functional tools. Whether you need a unique garden gate, custom fireplace tools, or a one-of-a-kind decorative piece, we bring your vision to life with uncompromising quality.

Our work has been recognized for its creative excellence and contribution to preserving traditional blacksmithing techniques in the local community.
